News Headlines Pakistan's Satellite Went To Moon

A small Pakistani satellite has imaged the sun and moon from lunar orbit, having taken a ride with China's Chang'e-6 mission to sample the far side of the moon. China launched Chang'e-6 on May 3, aiming to grab samples from Apollo crater on the lunar far side and deliver them to Earth for analysis.


News Headlines Pollution In Pakistan

Pakistan is the world's fourth most polluted country. Fine particulate air pollution (PM2. 5) shortens the average Pakistani resident's life expectancy by 3.9 years, relative to what it would be if the World Health Organization (WHO) guideline of 5 µg/m3 was met.
